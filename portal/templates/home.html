Welcome to the RNA-Explorer Portal

The RNA-Explorer Portal is designed to empower the scientific and bioinformatics community by providing access to extract and explore RNA sequences. Our platform enables researchers to analyze, and visualize genetic sequences with advanced computational tools.

Features

Extract Features

Analyze sequences by computing Sequential and Positional features like GC content, K-mer frequency, Mononucleotide composition and more with the help of UMAP.

Launch Extractor
Explore Features

Explore sequences with the help of RNA-FM foundation model embeddings and visualize sequence embedding with t-SNE plot.

Launch Explorer
Download Data

Download calculated sequence features or RNA-FM embeddings for further research and analysis.
